About

I'm a 19 years old panamanian programmer who studies in New Zealand and has managed to work on little yet challenging projects. Making music, games in Java, phaser.io, löve2D, Game Maker and designing websites are amongst my talents.

When it comes to programming, my personal goal is to successfully create, share and deploy useful services to the public, hoping the world can become a better place, one step at a time.
